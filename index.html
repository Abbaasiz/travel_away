All About Travel Away

Two brothers awe-struck by the beauty of Zanzibar, wanted more people to have an understanding of what an archipelago was and to be able to explore them all. The brothers have visited many archipelagos since and made long-lasting connections with the locals. Using their passion for teaching, their local relationships, and their strong belief in sharing wonderful experiences, the brothers decided to start Travel Away.
As a holiday services company, we strive to provide an unbeatable and unforgettable experience for all our customers. Situated in the (not so sunny) UK, we specialise in personalised packages for those wishing to explore the Archipelagos around the world. We help you book your flights; accommodation is provided and with optional full-boarding. Our excavations around the archipelagos are fully guided, with a choice of glamping or our wonderful chalets.
For those of you who seek to travel the world, we have specialist agents available to help you plan and book your trip, ensuring your safety and enjoyment.
